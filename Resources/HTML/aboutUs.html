About Us!

Welcome to Barter Basics! Barter Basics is an E-Commerce space based off of the barter system. In our space you can post items or services that you have up for trade and what items or services you are willing to accepts for your product. Our site is a community inclusive space that allows members to acquire goods without having to worry about the cost! Our mission is to reconstitute the idea of the community and creating a space where our members feel comfortable leaning on a neighbor when they're in need.
